🤝 Step 2: How to Vet a Wholesaler Before Listing
Before you list any seller on your platform, it's crucial to vet them to protect your brand's reputation. Here is a quick checklist:

Step 1: Check Their Reputation: Search for the business name online to find customer reviews and testimonials about their product quality and delivery reliability.

Step 2: Order a Sample: Before listing a seller’s entire catalog, buy one of their products yourself. This is the only way to truly test their shipping speed and product quality firsthand.

Step 3: Verify Company Legitimacy: You can check if a business is formally registered on the eCitizen portal under the Registrar of Companies.
